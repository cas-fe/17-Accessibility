Fixes

  • Visually hidden heading in each areas indicates its content.
  • Content areas use the semantically appropriate tags like <main> and <aside> (in combination with the corresponding ARIA roles role="main" and role="complementary").
  • Visually hidden but focusable skip navigation allows to jump to specific areas.
